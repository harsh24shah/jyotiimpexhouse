Buffalo Meat

Buffalo meat provides a great way for people to boost their intake of selenium. This is an antioxidant that helps prevent oxidative stress and reduces the increase of inflammation that the result of a poor diet.

Kadaknath Chicken

The Kadaknath is an Indian breed of chicken. Kadaknath chicken contains many kinds of amino acids , Vitamins B1, B2, B6, B12, C and E, niacin, protein, fat, calcium, phosphorus, iron, etc.

Kadaknth Eggs

The eggs of Kadaknth chickens can be used effectively to treat severe headaches, headaches after giving birth, faintness, asthma and nephritis (acute or chronic inflammation of the kidney).
